About Us

BiziNovate Accelerator is a dynamic social enterprise committed to bridging the digital divide and empowering individuals and communities through comprehensive technology education and IT services. With a passionate dedication to social impact, our mission is to raise awareness and provide education on the transformative power of technology in both business and personal life. We prioritize reaching underserved populations, particularly women and youth from disadvantaged communities, to ensure that everyone has equal access to opportunities in the digital age.

Mission Statement

BiziNovate Accelerator is dedicated to raising awareness and providing education on the importance of technology in both business and personal life. We aim to bridge the digital divide by empowering individuals from disadvantaged communities, especially women and youth, with the knowledge and skills needed to leverage technology for their success.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to create a world where everyone, regardless of background or gender, has equal access to and proficiency in technology, unlocking opportunities for personal growth, economic advancement, and social inclusion.

Our Services

Technology Awareness Workshops

Interactive workshops tailored to educate business owners and female youth on the benefits and applications of technology in various aspects of life, including business management, communication, education, and personal development.

Entrepreneurship Training

Training programs designed to equip aspiring entrepreneurs, especially women, with the skills and knowledge needed to start and grow their own businesses, leveraging technology as a key enabler.

Mentorship and Support

Providing mentorship and support networks to guide participants on their journey towards embracing technology and achieving their goals, including access to industry experts, networking events, and online communities.

High School Programs

Offering Digital Literacy and Robotics Training to High Schools, preparing students for future opportunities in technology and innovation.
